ASK SUPPORT FOR SERVICE PARTNER

“ Service partners are empanelled third-parties trained to provide specific services to Bizzexpo sellers. Service Partners will help Suppliers to
create their profile and list product professionally. ”

Note: Bizzexpo acts as a facilitator between sellers and service partners only.

If you are unhappy because the service performed by the
service partner has not been completed as agreed, and
you have raised this issue with the service partner but
have failed to resolve the same with the service partner,
then you may raise a claim with Bizzexpo under the
Service Assurance Programme.

You can ask for your refund within 30 days from the date of completion of the service by the service partner.
