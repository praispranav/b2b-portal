How to
respond to
buyer inquiries

Although most buyers purchase on a smaller scale
under the modern day B2B e-commerce climate,
responding to inquiries is still a crucial step in establishing
meaningful communication with a buyer.
Answering questions in detail and in a timely manner
is a must for converting inquiries into orders,
and making more sales .

Share with you several tips for
responding to inquiries

Respond to all inquiries within 24 hours in order to maintain a good response
rate and maximize your chances of winning new orders.

Double-check your grammar and spelling before sending your response to
make sure you look professional.

If possible, respond to the buyer during their working hours

Stay in touch by notifying them of promotions, new products or exhibitions
you will take part in and more.

Make sure you have high quality product pictures and videos as well as certificates
that you can share with interested buyers to show off your products
and professionalism.
