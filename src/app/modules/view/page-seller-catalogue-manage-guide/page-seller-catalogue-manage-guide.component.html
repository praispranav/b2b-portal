To start with Bizzexpo you should know the process of managing the catalogue . The below suggestions can help you keep a clean product catalog database:

To register an account on Bizzexpo please follow the steps below:

  • Go to Login page, click Join Free to register an account.

  • Enter your email address, tick the Agreement and Next.

  • A confirmation email will be sent to your mail. Please click the link in the email and fill in all the required information.

  • If you do not have a business, you can fill the “Company name” field with your name.

  • At your dashboard you will Supplier section to fill your all your company related details. Please fill all details carefully.

  • After filling all the information you can start posting your products into a bizzexpo category. You also have the option to create your own categories and product variants for your catalogue.

  • You should have good resolution pictures with the product description to place product with the product variants.

  • After the product posting you can wait for the product approval.
